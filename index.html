Profile

Full Name Marcie Van Auken

Age 27

Current City Brooklyn, NY

I am a graphic designer currently working in my chosen field. My alma mater is Northeastern University where I studied graphic design and had two 6-month co-operative experiences at both the Boston Globe doing editorial design, as well as at a small design firm called Metropolis Creative. I now have 4 years of professional experience since graduation and have an exceptional appreciation for all things design.

I have experience in all elements of print design but have been simultaneously finding a new place in my heart for web development. I am masterful in the Adobe Creative suite programs including InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator and Bridge. I have knowledge in HTML, CSS and JQuery.

I am currently…

working as the Digital Media Coordinator at Jacob & Co., located in Midtown NYC. I deal with all graphic work including but not limited to print design, web development and video editing. With my strong sense of organizational skills, I have reorganized our entire digital server database.

I am also currently attending a 16-week certificate program in Web Development at the New York Code + Design Academy where I will further my love, appreciation and knowledge of web programming.
